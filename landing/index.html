under the hood

it just works.

auto-extraction.

commit reads new messages and finds commitments automatically. who said what, who owes what, with the original quote.

auto-resolution.

when a commitment is fulfilled in the conversation — a file shared, a task confirmed — commit marks it done. no manual work.

follow-ups.

things they owe you with no reply. commit surfaces them, drafts a polite nudge, and lets you send it in one tap.

reminders.

set a reminder on any commitment. when it's due, commit sends you a WhatsApp message so it surfaces right in your chat list.

favorites.

on privacy

your chats, your machine.

stays on your machine

commit connects directly to WhatsApp's servers using the same protocol as WhatsApp Web. messages are decrypted and processed locally. nothing is stored on a cloud server.

leaves your machine

message content is sent to the Claude API for commitment analysis. Anthropic does not train on API inputs — their data retention policy governs what happens on their side. no human review.

to fully remove: unlink the device from WhatsApp, delete the app, and delete ~/.commit/ from your home folder.
